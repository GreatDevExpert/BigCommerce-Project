Smart Cleanse Manager

Desiree Roberts is General Manager of Smart Cleanse. Her main focus is on empowering individuals to reclaim their health and wellbeing. Through the Smart Cleanse 14 day detox program, complimented by advanced coaching and mentoring, Desiree aims to help people achieve their full potential. Desiree has dedicated her life and work to the study and practice of health, vitality and wellbeing. She combines knowledge of health and fitness with her passion for assisting people to create happy and balanced lives.
